This program teaches someone to make a ham sandwich

open the fridge
get ham from the inside the fridge
close the fridge
open the pantry
Grab bread from pantry
close the pantry
remove two slices of bread and place both slices next to each other horizontality
remove two slices of ham from the package and place both on top of each other on one slice of bread
put the slice of bread without ham on top of the slice of bread with ham

open fridge
put away the ham
close fridge
open pantry
put away the bread
close pantry

Enjoy your sandwich
